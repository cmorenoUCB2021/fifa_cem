Which FIFA Confederation has the strongest average performance?

FIFA is an international governing body of association football, beach football and futsal. It was founded in 1904. FIFA divides the countries in six regions (AFC, CAF, CONCACAF, CONMEBOL, OFC, UEFA). Each confederation implement an eliminatory process to define the countries representing them in the Wold Cup. Wold Cups are implemented every 4 year, and there have been 21 world cups within 1930 and 2018. Economically, EUFA is the strongest confederation with the most money. The following graphs explore the performance of the confederations in the World Cups. The FIFA confederations are the following:

  1. AFC = Asian Football Confederation in Asia and Australia.
  2. CAF = Confédération Africaine de Football in Africa.
  3. CONCACAF = Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Association Football in North America and Central America.
  4. CONMEBOL = Confederación Sudamericana de Fútbol
  5. OFC = Oceania Football Confederation in Oceania
  6. UEFA = Union of European Football Associations
